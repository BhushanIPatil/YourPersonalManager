About Us

Simple, powerful tools for planning and productivity

What We Offer

Daily Activity Planner is a collection of free, browser-based tools designed to help you stay organized. No signup, no installation—everything runs directly in your browser. Your data stays on your device.

Our Tools

  • Daily Activity Planner — Plan your day with activities, time slots, and notes. Export to PDF or upload and edit existing plans.
  • Travel Manager — Track travel expenses, accommodation, transport, itinerary, and generate professional PDF reports.
  • Project Planner — Manage project work with task IDs, status tracking, and work done records. Export clean PDF reports.
