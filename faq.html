Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

General

Do I need to create an account?

No. All our tools work without signup or login. Just open the page and start planning.

Is my data stored somewhere?

No. Your data stays in your browser. We don’t have servers that store your plans, travel details, or project tasks. Only you see your content.

Does it work offline?

Yes, after the first load. Your browser caches the page and scripts. You can plan and generate PDFs without an internet connection once everything is loaded.

Is it free?

Yes. Daily Activity Planner, Travel Manager, and Project Planner are free to use. There are no premium features or hidden costs.

PDF Features

Can I edit a PDF after generating it?

Yes. Upload the PDF you generated (via the upload area on each tool’s page). We extract the content, fill the form, and you can edit and regenerate a new PDF.

Will it work with PDFs from other apps?

Upload and edit works best with PDFs created by our tools. They include metadata we use to reconstruct the form. PDFs from other apps may not parse correctly.

What format are the PDFs?

Standard A4 PDFs. You can open them in any PDF reader, print them, or share them. They’re designed to be readable and professional.

Tools

What’s the difference between the three tools?

Daily Activity Planner is for day-to-day schedules (time, activities, notes). Travel Manager is for trips (expenses, accommodation, transport, itinerary). Project Planner is for projects (tasks, status, work records). Each generates a tailored PDF.

Can I use this on mobile?

Yes. The site is responsive. Forms and layouts adapt to smaller screens. PDF generation works on supported mobile browsers.

What browsers are supported?

Modern browsers: Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge. JavaScript must be enabled. For PDF upload and edit, your browser needs to support the File API and related features.
