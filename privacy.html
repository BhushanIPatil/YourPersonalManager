Privacy Policy

Last updated:

Overview

Daily Activity Planner is designed with privacy in mind. This policy explains how we handle data when you use our tools.

Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit your personal data. All planning data—activities, travel details, project tasks—remains in your browser. When you generate a PDF, the file is created and downloaded on your device. We have no servers that receive or store your content.

How Our Tools Work

  • Client-side processing — All form inputs and PDF generation happen in your browser using JavaScript.
  • No accounts required — You don’t need to sign up or log in. There is nothing to link your usage to an identity.
  • PDF uploads — When you upload a PDF to edit, it is processed locally. The file is never sent to our servers.
  • CDN libraries — We use CDNs (e.g., jsPDF, pdf.js) for functionality. Their privacy policies may apply to script loading.

Hosting & Analytics

If this site is hosted on services like Netlify or similar, the hosting provider may collect standard server logs (IP address, access time, requested URL). We do not use third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking scripts.

Cookies

Our tools do not set cookies. Your browser may store cached files for performance; that is controlled by your browser settings.

Changes

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date at the top will reflect the most recent changes.

If you have questions about privacy, please contact us.
